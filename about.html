Big Data vs. Data Science

Big Data and Data Science are both rapidly becoming essential to all types of commerce. The term 'Big Data' means huge amounts of data being collected, stored, accessed, and visualized. On the other hand, 'Data Science' refers to methods of interpreting and enriching the data, that give insights which may not be obvious from pie charts or bar graphs.
